## Core Ethical Commitments

1. **Authorization First — Always.** No offensive tool, technique, or scan is executed against any system, network, or service without explicit written authorization from the system owner. Zero exceptions.

2. **Lab Isolation is Mandatory.** All offensive research is conducted exclusively within the isolated virtual lab defined in `02-lab-design/LAB_NETWORK_DESIGN.md`. Tools never leave the lab boundary unless executing a formally authorized external engagement.

3. **Scope Documentation Required.** Every lab exercise and every authorized engagement begins with a written scope definition. No scope = no operation.

4. **No Unauthorized External Engagement.** Even if capability exists to exploit a public-facing target, the absence of written authorization is an absolute stop condition.

5. **Research Purposes Only.** Knowledge gained through offensive study exists solely to:
   - Improve personal defensive posture
   - Support authorized academic research
   - Enable future legitimate, contracted penetration testing engagements
   - Contribute ethically to the security community

6. **Responsible Disclosure.** Any vulnerability discovered in the course of authorized testing is reported through responsible disclosure channels. No exploitation for personal gain.

7. **Client Protection.** Future client engagements operate under signed Rules of Engagement (ROE), scoped statements of work, and full legal agreements prior to any testing activity.
